RULES

  • The participants must register as a team of two.
  • Each team will have seven minutes to cross the maze.
  • Within these 7 minutes, each team will have to cross the maze from start to finish while going through each of the seven checkpoints and the fun games counter situated at different locations within the maze.
  • At these checkpoints, teams will have to answer certain questions.
  • At the fun games counter, the teams will have to take part in a few quick and fun games to gain points.
  • Teams will be awarded points based on the number of checkpoints crossed and how they perform at each checkpoint within the stipulated time limit.
  • In the end, the total points of each team will be calculated and the top three teams with the most points will be the winners
